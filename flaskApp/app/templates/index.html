How to start creating with Scavy App

Getting Started

  • 1. Create an account
  • 2. Build a scavenger hunt game
  • 3. Publish your completed game on your account
  • 4. Play and share your game code with friends!

Questions? We have answers.



Scavy App is free for all users!

There is no limit on the number of games you can create. You must have a Scavy App account to create hunts.

You can share your unique game code for private or public games. Find your game code on your account page & click your game name!

On the play tab, you can filter Scavy App games by name, location, and most played.

You can play games as a guest. You do not have to create an account to play.
